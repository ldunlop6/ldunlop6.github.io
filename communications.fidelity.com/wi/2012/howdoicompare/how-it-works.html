How it works

We don't pick stocks or try to time the market. We cut out the noise and focus on building your long-term savings. It's not sexy, but it works.

  1. Decide how much money you want to invest

    Start with a chunk of money or invest monthly. Don’t worry, you can take out your money any time you like.

  2. We'll help you pick a personalized portfolio

    Our sign-up is super simple. Build a diversified portfolio of low-fee funds in minutes.

  3. Go live your life

    Everything is automated – from rebalancing to dividend reinvestment, even tax efficiency – so you stay on track.

  4. Get advice anytime

    Call, text or email us at any time for free on-demand advice.
