We're Hiring

We’ve put together an impressive founding team to change the way all Canadians invest their savings. Wealthsimple is backed by some of the biggest names in technology and finance, and we’re in a great position to grow quickly and disrupt the financial industry. We look for talented people who want to work in a fast-paced environment.We’re a small team, which means you’ll have more responsibility and chances to learn than many other companies.

Our office is located in Toronto’s vibrant Queen West neighborhood, surrounded by great dining, shopping, and entertainment. Our office features 20-foot ceilings, mid-century modern furniture, hardwood floors, natural light, a newly-renovated kitchen, and an old-fashioned library/meeting room.

Perks & Benefits

Savings Matching Plan

Wealthsimple offers a generous savings matching program to a Wealthsimple account of your choosing.

Healthcare

Wealthsimple offers top-tier health, dental and vision insurance so the team can focus on work.

Your Workspace

Every employee receives their own desk and ergonomic chair. You are also allowed to build your own tech setup. You can work with the computer and monitor of your choice. Most choose Macbook Air or Pro and Apple Thunderbolt Display.

Lunch and Snacks

Wealthsimple provides healthy, delicious and free lunch each day, as well as endless snacks and beverages.

Other benefits

  • Have a real impact on the direction of our product 
  • Develop and implement new technical processes 
  • Have a hand in building the next big thing in financial technology
  • Paid-time off and holidays
  • Bike Share Toronto membership
  • Professional development budget

We pride ourselves on creating a collaborative, diverse, and inclusive work place. Candidates who identify as a minority are welcome and encouraged to apply!
